About Our Foundation

The Virtuous Vision Ladies Foundation is a compassionate organization dedicated to supporting widows and the ill in our communities. Located in Takoradi behind Absa Bank, Ako Addie Street, we work tirelessly to provide essential resources, emotional support, and financial assistance to those who need it most.

Our mission is rooted in the belief that everyone deserves dignity, care, and support during difficult times. We channel the generosity of donors to make a tangible difference in the lives of widows and the ill, ensuring they receive the help they need to navigate life's challenges.

Through our various programs and initiatives, we aim to create a supportive community where widows can find solace, resources, and the strength to rebuild their lives with hope and dignity.

Make a Difference

Your generous donations enable us to continue our vital work supporting widows and the ill in our communities. Every contribution, no matter the size, makes a significant impact on someone's life.

We accept various forms of donations including monetary contributions, food items, clothing, and other essential supplies. Your support helps us provide hope and practical assistance to those who need it most.

Join us in our mission to bring comfort and support to widows and the ill. Together, we can make a lasting difference in our communities.
